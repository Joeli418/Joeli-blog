Gift Guide

I do not expect gift from people. However, any gift is a sign of thought and I will appreciate it.

If you are trying to get me a right gift, this is just a guide on good gifts to get for me :)


Gifts I prefer

I'll save you some thinking; The best gift you could get me is here. If you got it, it'd be a surprise.

I have everything I want. If you really insist on getting me something, I would appreciate :
  • A donation to World Food Program.
  • A donation to Animals Australia.
  • Any symbolic animal adoption
  • Or simply a nice card.

Gifts that you shouldn't get - I will not appreciate

  • Don't get me any meat or animal product such as leather
  • Don't buy me any pets or gift that forces me to take responsibility
  • Don't get things that violates my moral values
  • Don't pick something from the trash can and give it to me.

Gifts that are less preferred

  • Try not to get me clothings.
  • Try not to give me food.
  • If you know I have something, try not to get something for me to replace it.
  • If you are planning to give me books, just know that I have a Kindle. You should ask me first and a digital copy.
