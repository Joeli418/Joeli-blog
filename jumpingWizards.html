This game should be played on a laptop without keyboard ghosting.

Player 1 controls with ad to move, w to jump, e to shoot right and q to shoot left.
Player 2 controls with jl to move, i to jump, o to shoot right and u to shoot left.
Deal damage to opponent either by shooting them or by jumping on them.

You can only shoot one bullet at a time. Hold down the jump to increase jump height
